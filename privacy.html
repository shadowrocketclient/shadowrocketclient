Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Introduction

Shadowrocketclient (hereinafter referred to as "we", "us", or "our") takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy is intended to explain how we handle (or do not handle) your data when you use the Shadowrocket application (hereinafter referred to as "the App").

In short: We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data or network traffic data.

Data Collection and Usage

The App is designed to provide you with secure network proxy services. To achieve this, we need to process your network traffic, but all data processing is performed locally on your device only.

1. Personal Information

We do not collect any of your personally identifiable information (such as name, email, phone number, etc.).

2. Network Traffic Data

To provide VPN and proxy services, the App needs to intercept and forward your network requests locally. This data flows only within your device's memory and is never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers.

3. Server Configuration and Subscriptions

The server configurations, subscription links, and rule lists you add are stored locally on your device (or synchronized via your iCloud account). We do not have access to your configuration information.

Data Security

Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of data leakage. All your configuration information is protected by the iOS system sandbox mechanism.

Third-Party Services

The App does not contain any third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics, Fabric, etc.) or advertising SDKs.
